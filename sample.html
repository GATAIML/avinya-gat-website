Learning management system (LMS)

Enhance your institution's teaching capabilities by adding new subjects, tracking lesson plans related to various subjects, and managing the content teachers will deliver in each subject. Enable a seamless learning experience for students.

Admission management

Streamline the process of tracking all admissions for your educational institute, from preschool to university. Manage applications, admissions data, and applicant information efficiently.

Fee management

Effectively monitor and manage fees related to students. Create, track, and manage various fee structures, keep tabs on payments, and easily identify students with pending fees. Additionally, track fee receipts and revenue.

Exam management

Create and manage exams effortlessly while tracking students' performance in specific exams. Easily access and analyze exam results to support continuous improvement in academic standards.

Library management

Efficiently manage your institution's library, from tracking students who have borrowed books to identifying those who have yet to return them. Keep a record of library inventory and circulation.

Hostel management

Monitor hostel activities seamlessly—from adding students to rooms and managing menu details to tracking the presence of students in the hostel. Ensure a safe and comfortable environment for your residential students.

Transportation management

Keep track of all vehicles associated with your institution, including mapping students to their respective transport services. Monitor the status and location of each vehicle in real time.

Entry/exit management

Enhance security by tracking visitors who enter your institute. This module also helps monitor hostel students as they come and go, including recording entry and exit times.

Communications

Effectively manage and send communications to parents, students, and staff. Streamline the process of sharing information, updates, and announcements.

Service request and inventory

Enable teachers to submit service and asset requests seamlessly. The inventory administrator can add assets and efficiently monitor all requests, ensuring a well-maintained and functional learning environment.

Events management

Keep track of all events related to your institution, such as inter-school competitions. Monitor student participation and manage logistics, including teacher supervision. This module simplifies communication with students and staff for event-related updates.

Alumni management

Maintain a comprehensive database of former students who have studied at your educational institute. This module allows you to stay connected with alumni and track their post-graduation achievements and contributions.

Q&A

Maintain a comprehensive database of questions that will appear on exams. This module will help you organize and categorize questions related to all subjects, making exam preparation more efficient.

Portals for every user persona

Personalized portals offer multiple perspectives with customized access to information and services. This helps enhance communication, transparency, and communication amongst all stakeholders.

  • Admin portal

    Oversee your institute's operations with an intuitive, user-friendly dashboard that houses all your administrative information in one place. Get rid of time-consuming, paper-laden manual processes with built-in automation tools.

  • Student portal

    Students can now view their schedules, check attendance records, access course materials, and submit assignments. It's a one-stop platform for them to manage their academic life effectively.

  • Faculty portal

    Staff can access their portal to manage courses, update lesson plans, track student progress, and communicate with students. It simplifies administrative tasks and allows them to focus on what matters most—teaching.
